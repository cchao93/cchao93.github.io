About

Hi! My name is Victor. I am a computer science & economics major at Tufts University, expecting to graduate in May 2016. Most of my past projects and work experience involved full-stack web development, but I am also passionate about system-level programming and data science.

Outside of work and the classroom, I am an avid musician. Having played the piano since I was six, I went through classical training until eighth grade, when I stepped into the enchanting world of percussion. From pop to punk rock to jazz, I have been exposed to various genres and played in numerous bands. Born and raised in Taiwan, I also enjoy cultural exploration and travel.

If anything sounds interesting to you, feel free to get in touch with me.
