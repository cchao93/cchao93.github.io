About

I am a computer science & economics double major at Tufts University in Medford, MA, expecting to graduate in May 2016. I am currently seeking opportunities in software development, data science, and IT consulting. Most of my past projects and work experience involved web application development, but I have also done some work in OS-level development, distributed systems, networking, and more recently, machine learning. I look forward to exploring any of the above in the future.

Outside of work and the classroom, I am an avid musician. Having played the piano since I was six, I went through formal classical training until eighth grade, when I stepped into the enchanting world of percussion. From pop to punk rock to jazz, I have been exposed to various genres and played in numerous bands. Born and raised in Taiwan, I also enjoy cultural exploration and travel.

If anything sounds interesting to you, feel free to get in touch with me.
